Glossary & Definitions

Base Month

The most recent month of actual data provided in your raw CSV/Excel upload. All forecasts are built by carrying this month forward.

Headcount (Unique IDs)

The system counts the unique Employee IDs within a timeframe. If an employee is split across two accounts, they only count as 1 Headcount. In YTD views, an employee who existed in Jan and Feb is still only counted as 1 for the YTD Total.

Financial Year (FY)

The UWI Financial Year runs from August 1st through July 31st. YTD and Annual groupings automatically conform to this schedule.

Variance

Calculated as `Current Period - Previous Period`. A positive variance (Red) indicates higher costs or more employees. A negative variance (Green) indicates lower costs or fewer employees.

Flat Increment vs One-Off

A Flat Increment adds a specific dollar amount to an employee's pay from the chosen start month onwards forever. A One-Off payment adds a dollar amount to only that specific month.
